About Alexa

After graduating high school, Alexa spent several years working at BriarPatch Co-op as a Computer Information Systems Technician. This inspired her to continue her studies and enroll Sierra College in Grass Valley where she discovered her true passion of coding.

Thoroughly enjoying her time at Sierra College Alexa took as many classes as she could leading her to discover a love for American Sign Language(ASL) & Deaf Studies. In order to become proficient in ASL Alexa has volunteered at Hands & Voices family camp 2015 and continues to involve herself with the Deaf community.

Alexa recently graduated from Sierra College with a degree in Natural Science and enrolled in California State University Sacramento where is she pursuing both her interests Majoring in Computer Science and Minoring in Deaf Studies.
